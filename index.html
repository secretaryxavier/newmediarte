Mechanisms of Hate Speech: Battling the Power of Digital Hate

Understanding how digital platforms fuel and fight hate

Introduction

What is hate speech? Hate speech is offensive discourse that targets a group or an individual based on inherent characteristics such as race, religion, or gender.

Why does it matter? Hate speech is a pervasive issue that infiltrates many aspects of daily life and significantly impacts people's outlook, both online and offline. Well-known figures like Kanye West and Elon Musk have been associated with hate speech or inflammatory actions, highlighting the influence and reach of digital hate.
